## Register Selection Guide

When deciding which register to use:

1. **Check the channel.** Some channels have a default register (e.g., LinkedIn posts → default, client emails → [register name]).
2. **Check the audience.** Writing to prospects vs. existing clients vs. internal team may use different registers.
3. **Check the purpose.** Informing vs. persuading vs. supporting may call for different registers.
4. **When in doubt, use the default.** The base voice works everywhere. Registers are refinements, not requirements.
