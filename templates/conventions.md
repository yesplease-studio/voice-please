## Writing Rules

Principles that guide all content. Each stated as a rule with a brief rationale.

[Start with the rules that matter most for this company. Below is a starter set -- adopt, modify, or replace based on your voice profile and judgment.]

**Short sentences. Use them.**
Long explanations signal that the idea isn't sharp enough yet. One clear sentence beats three hedged ones.

**Specific over vague.**
Name the exact problem, the exact customer, the exact moment. Generic descriptions say nothing.

**Human-first.**
Talk about people before talking about software or products. Users, customers, decision-makers come before features.

**Name the problem before the solution.**
Every good piece of content starts with a recognition -- something the reader has felt but hasn't fully articulated.
