## Hierarchical Inheritance

[For companies using nested CLAUDE.md files or multi-project setups.]

Voice configurations compose in layers. Each layer inherits from above and can override specific elements:

| Level | What it contains | Overrides |
|-------|-----------------|-----------|
| **Root** | Full voice system (this file) | None -- this is the base |
| **Company/Project** | Project-specific register selection, audience adjustments | Register defaults, audience language focus |
| **Per-task** | Channel-specific format templates, tone adjustments | Format templates, energy level |

**Rule:** Lower levels can adjust tone and select registers, but cannot override personality, hard rules, or banned phrases. Those are set at the root level.
